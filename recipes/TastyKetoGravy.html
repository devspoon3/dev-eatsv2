Tasty Keto Gravy

This amazing recipe will have you feeling full and fullfilled without a heavy digestion

The most savory topping you can make and you can make as much volume as you like by doubling or even tripling the amount.

Ingredients

  • Leftover Grease from cooking protein preferably a juicy steak
  • 3-4 Tbs Grassfed Butter
  • Beef Bone Broth
  • Startch i.e. Coconut flour or Xanthum Gum

Steps

  1. 1. Cook your protein of choice over a pan with 1-2 tbsp of Grassfed butter and season to your taste. I like to use cast Iron for this.
  2. 2. After it's finished remove protein item from the pan leaving as much of the grease or remnants on the pan as possible
  3. 3. Next, bring the pan to low-med head and add the reamining amount of butter and slowly mix in some bone broth
  4. 4. Stir gently and slowly add in some of your starch of choice
  5. 5. Finally, once everything starts to thicken up nicely and you can scrape a spatula along the bottom and nothing sticks to the pan you are done. Place into a little gravy or sauce bowl and serve :)

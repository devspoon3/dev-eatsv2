Lion's Mane Marinade

  • 1/4 avocado oil or olive oil
  • 1 Tsp of Cumin
  • 1 Tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 cup of Cilantro
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 cup of orange juice
  • 4 cloves crushed garlic
  • 3 tbsp vegan Worcerstershire sauce
  • Few drops of liquid smoke
  • 1/4 Tbsp of coconut aminos

Steps

  1. Step 1. Chop and Prepare Lion's Mane
  2. Step 2. Mix olive oil, cumin, chili powder, cilantro, lime juice, oranges juice, garlic, worcestershire sauce, liquid smoke coconut aminos, salt & pepper to taste.
  3. Step 3. Marinate Lions Mane in the sauce. The longer it marinates, the better.
  4. Step 4. Drain, Grill, and Serve.

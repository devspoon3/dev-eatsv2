PanSearedChardorCollard

This is my go to snack during the day

Quinch your tastebuds with a tasty savory green treat. Sub seasonings of your choice and switch it up if you'd like.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Large Collard Leaves
  • Butter, Coconut Oil or Olive Oil
  • Salt and/or Jamaican Jerk Seasoning

Steps

  1. Take the Leaves and stack them on top of eachother in the same direction
  2. Gently hold them down with one hand and slice into strips
  3. Put butter or oil of choice into Pan and turn on med-low heat to melt or distribute evenly
  4. Put Collard or Chard onto the heated coated pan
  5. Gently toss and sear the greens until cooked throughout approx 5 min
  6. Plate and serve as a side or a delicious mid day snack
